# Overview

Scale your product with confidence. We'll empower your platform with Tactna’s unified identity foundation.

Every feature is purpose-built to deliver security, flexibility, and governance—the pillars of enterprise-grade scalability.

## Core Capabilities

Tactna provides a complete suite of identity and access management features designed for modern SaaS environments.


### Authentication

Delegate authentication to your preferred Identity Provider (IdP) — supporting both **Auth0** and **Amazon Cognito** — while keeping full control of user experience and data integrity.

Highlights:

- Multiple Sign-in Methods
- Social & Enterprise Login (SAML, OIDC Federation)
- Adaptive Authentication (Risk-based Access Control)
- API-based Authentication (Client Credentials Flow, M2M)
- Session & Token Management (JWT, Refresh Token, SSO, SLO)
- Custom Auth Flows (Auth0 Triggers and Actions)

[Learn more about Authentication](./features/authentication)

### Authorization

Define how access should be granted, not just who gets it.
Tactna enables fine-grained, team-aware access control.

Highlights:

- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- Attribute-Based Access Control (ABAC)
- Resource-level Permissions across pages, features, and data
- Delegated Access & Impersonation for administrative and support operations

 [Learn more about Authorization](./features/authorization)


### User Management

Tactna provides a unified and secure way to manage users across applications, teams, and tenants.
From identity linking to status control, every capability is designed to give enterprises full visibility and governance over their user base—without compromising developer flexibility.

Highlights:
- Centralized User Directory & Profile Management
- Identity Linking and Federation with external IdPs
- User Activation, Suspension, and Custom Status


### Team Management (for Multi-Tenant SaaS)

A “team” in Tactna represents any collaborative unit—whether it’s a **company**, **department**, **family**, or **community**.
Tactna provides a unified framework for managing complex organizational structures, giving you precise control over memberships, roles, and governance.

**Highlights:**

* Centralized Team Directory & Profile Management
* Membership & Invitation Management
* Team Self-Management for delegated administration
* Team Policies & Hierarchical Governance
* Team Lifecycle Control (Activate, Suspend, Archive)


### Cross-App Identity Sharing

Tactna provides a unified identity layer that enables seamless authentication and access across multiple applications within your enterprise digital ecosystem.
By centralizing identity at the platform level, users can sign in once and securely access all authorized applications—reducing friction while maintaining strict boundaries and governance for each app.

**Highlights:**

* Single Sign-On across multiple SaaS applications
* Unified session and token management
* Centralized identity governance across apps
* Team-level isolation and policy enforcement
* Simplified provisioning and deprovisioning


### Customization

Tactna is designed to adapt to your unique business requirements and branding needs.
From custom workflows and attributes to embedded UI components, every aspect of the platform can be tailored to your exact specifications.

**Highlights:**

* Custom Workflows such as sign-up and app activation. Build your own screen transitions and backend processes to match your business requirements
* Custom Attributes to extend user, team, and per-application data models
* Embedded UI Components to extend portal, sign-up, and account migration experiences
* Role & Permission Customization for fine-grained access control
* Custom Email Templates
* Custom Domain support
